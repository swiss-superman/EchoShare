# Sustainability
Repository for BGS 24 Hour Hackathon.


# Idea
A community based social networking like web app designed for individuals to post, organize and work to tackle the problem of pollution near water bodies(beaches, ponds etc).

Points:
    1. Community driven app - Individuals post geotagged photos of litter around water bodies.
    2. The intensity of litter around a given water body is shown using heatmap on a google map.
    3. Users can login with Google SSO or Apple SSO for best authentication.
